## Decision summary

Two phases by **evaluation depth** (D1): `n < min_exploitation_windows` (default 5) is Exploration and
is **never persisted** (guards early lucky candidates); `n ≥ min` graduates to Exploitation. A candidate
persists (D2) iff graduated, finite, and **robustly above the bar** — lower confidence bound
`mean − k_sigma·se ≥ threshold`. The threshold is the **spec A1 baseline** derived from the full
validation distribution (D3, `Quantile(0.75)` default); the stricter **train/CV-only** anti-leakage
alternative (D4) is documented and parameter-ready (`from_distribution` is source-agnostic), not enabled.
