## Decision summary

`capacity(profile, model) = (gross_edge·(1−edge_retention) − turnover·half_spread) /
(impact_coeff·turnover²)` — the AUM at which size-dependent impact erodes net edge to its retained floor;
falls quadratically in turnover (guards: `0` uneconomic, `+∞` no size impact). `cap_weights` water-fills
the unit budget so `weight_i ≤ capacity_i/target_aum`, redistributing capped slack; capacity-constrained
excess stays uninvested. Impact form is QE-109's, re-parameterised in `CapacityModel` rather than importing
`qe_wfo::friction` (firewall: no `qe-wfo` dep).
