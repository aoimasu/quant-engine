## Decision summary

New crate `qe-gate` (deps only `qe-validation` + `serde`). `split_with_embargo(n, holdout_len, embargo)`
carves disjoint `train | embargo | holdout` blocks (holdout = final OOS slice, embargo = purged gap).
`evaluate_g1` applies the pre-registered criteria on the holdout — sufficient holdout size, net-of-cost
edge persists (`≥` floor), DSR `>` threshold, SPA p-value `<` alpha, OOS Sharpe `≥ IS·(1−tol)` — and
promotes iff **all** pass. `G1Decision { promoted, criteria: Vec<CriterionResult> }` is serde — the
auditable pass/fail-with-evidence record; `failed_criteria()` lists blockers.

## Review (pass 1) — Approved

Careful principal-level pass (final P1 gate, blocks Phase 2). Verdict: **Approved.** No real bugs.

- **(a) Four criteria — directions/boundaries correct.** edge `≥` floor; DSR `>` threshold; SPA `p < α`;
  OOS `holdout_sharpe ≥ IS·(1−tol)` (one-sided, product form avoids div-by-zero). None inverted;
  `sharpe_ratio(holdout_returns)` is the right net-of-cost edge metric.
- **(b) Conjunctive promotion** (`all(passed)`); the isolation test is sound — "edge gone" trips criteria
  1 & 4 (so `contains`), DSR/SPA/OOS cases keep the strong holdout and flip one input (so `==`).
- **(c) Criterion 4 sound** incl. the negative-IS interaction (criterion 1 independently guards the
  absolute edge; the `IS·(1−tol)` floor bites exactly where over-fit lives — large positive IS collapsing
  OOS). The `inflated_is·4` test genuinely exercises it.
- **(d) `split_with_embargo`** partitions `0..n` (contiguous, disjoint, holdout exact `min(holdout_len,n)`);
  the `(10,8,5)` clamp prioritises the protected holdout. No panic (saturating arithmetic).
- **(e) Evidence** recorded (name/passed/value/threshold per criterion; serde round-trip;
  `failed_criteria()`). **(f) Firewall** clean (only `qe-validation` + `serde`; QE-132 guard still green).

Three non-blocking notes: (1) criterion-1 zero boundary (configurable pre-registration choice); (2)
degenerate/empty holdout could pass criteria 1 & 4 vacuously — **addressed** in pass 2; (3) negative-IS
criterion-4 leniency (safe, criterion 1 guards the edge).
