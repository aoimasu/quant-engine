## Decision summary

Fitness = ergodic **time-average log-growth** `mean ln(1+r)` of net-of-cost per-period returns (D1);
near-ruin absorbing (`r ≤ −1` ⇒ `−∞`); `geom_return` is the human-readable compound rate; compounding
resolution fixed/consistent (D2). Noise-robust evaluation summarises per-window growth as mean ±
standard error (D3); `should_replace` displaces an elite only when a challenger clears `k_sigma`
combined SEs (D4) — no churn on noise. CV = **purged + embargoed** folds (D5): `purge = lookback +
label_horizon`, one-sided embargo after each test block (default = max lookback). The leakage-free
invariant `|tr − te| > lookback + label_horizon` holds for every (train, test) pair.

## Review (pass 1) — Approved

Principal-level review of `aad9227`. All three ACs met; fitness/SE math correct; purge/embargo invariant
re-derived by hand. No real bugs.

- **(a) Fitness math.** `log_growth = mean ln(1+r)` with `1+r ≤ 0` ⇒ `−∞` guard (catches `r=−1` exactly
  and `r<−1` NaN); `geom_return = exp(·)−1`. Volatility-drag round-trip pinned; near-ruin + fixed
  compounding-resolution documented.
- **(b) Noise-robust eval.** `std_error = sqrt(var/n)` with `n−1` sample-variance divisor = `sample_sd/√n`;
  `n<2`/non-finite ⇒ SE 0. `should_replace` refuses within-band churn, handles ruin both ways, degenerates
  to strict-greater at `n=1`.
- **(c) Leakage-free CV.** Re-derived the invariant from the exclusion zone `[t_start−purge,
  t_end+purge+embargo)`: kept-left `te−tr ≥ purge+1`, kept-right `tr−te ≥ purge+embargo+1` ⇒
  `|tr−te| ≥ L+H+1 > L+H` always; integer windows disjoint iff `|tr−te| > L+H`. No off-by-one; one-sided
  embargo matches López de Prado. AC fixture is a genuine all-pairs/all-folds proof; embargo documented +
  pinned (`embargo == lookback`).
- **(d) Naive-k-fold contrast.** Same machinery, `purge=embargo=0` vs real `L=5,H=2` fails on adjacent
  bars (concrete bar-23/bar-24). Valid.
