### Goal
Enforce QE-009 hard caps before targets leave the planner: max notional, max leverage, gross/net caps,
liquidation-distance floor, margin-utilisation ceiling. Clamp or halt per contract.

### Acceptance criteria (from backlog)
- [x] A target implying an unsafe liquidation distance or breaching a cap is clamped/halted, not sent —
  `unsafe_liquidation_distance_is_rejected`, `oversized_notional_is_clamped`, `excess_leverage_is_clamped`,
  `gross_and_net_and_margin_breaches_reject`, `reject_outranks_clamp`, `degenerate_capital_is_handled_safely`.

### Implementation summary
- `PreTradeGovernor::check(target, capital)` tests each configured cap → `LimitBreach`es (via
  `with_default_outcome`) → `PreTradeVerdict` by outcome severity (`Halt` > `Reject` > `Clamp`/`Send`). Clamp
  (notional/leverage) shrinks to the tightest cap (sign preserved); Reject (gross/net, liq floor, margin
  ceiling) refuses (no target sent, keep trading); Halt = contract-general severity path (DrawdownCap→Halt is
  the QE-212 breaker / QE-216 kill path, out of scope).
- Liquidation distance `E/|notional| − mmr`; margin util `(|notional|×mmr)/available` (venue mmr). All
  divisions guarded; documented venue-agnostic approximations.
