## Implementation
Filled the `qe-domain` crate: `money` (Price/Qty/Notional over `rust_decimal`, exact add/sub, single
rounding point `Price::notional`), `instrument` (InstrumentId canonicalised, Venue), `time`
(Timestamp epoch-ms, TimeInterval half-open), `resolution` (single shared bar-resolution enum), `bar`
(OHLCVT with `low ≤ {open,close} ≤ high`), `funding` (signed FundingRate + sample), `side`
(Side/Direction total conversions), `vintage` (64-hex VintageHash). Validation runs on construction
**and** at the serde boundary.

## Review outcome
**Approved** (round 2).

- **Round 1 — [Reviewed], one blocker.** The reviewer demonstrated (with crafted JSON) that the
  validating constructors were bypassed at the (de)serialization & public-field boundary: a negative
  `Price`, un-canonicalised `InstrumentId`, invalid `VintageHash`, reversed `TimeInterval`, and an
  OHLC-invalid `Bar` (`range() = -5`) all deserialized `Ok`. AC #1's money exactness and non-vacuous
  proptests were verified positively; the blocker was the invariant bypass.
- **Round 2 — [Approved].** Fixed on every path: validating `Deserialize` for `Price`/`Qty`;
  `#[serde(try_from = "String")]` → `new` for `InstrumentId`/`VintageHash`;
  `#[serde(try_from = "TimeIntervalWire")]` for `TimeInterval`; `Bar` made private-fields-with-getters
  plus a validating `BarWire`/`#[serde(into)]` (exact round-trip preserved). Added
  `deserialize_rejects_*` tests for every type. Reviewer re-ran all round-1 probes — all now `Err` —
  and confirmed canonicalisation-on-deserialize and exact `Bar` serde round-trip. Two minor notes
  resolved: documented `Price::notional`'s 28-digit precondition; added `Notional` overflow tests.
