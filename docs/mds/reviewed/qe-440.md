Consistent with QE-107 causal discipline (ADV is an execution-instant frame that legitimately includes the completed `volume[i]`). No peek.
2. **`powd` determinism on the sealed path (CRITICAL) — verified at crate source.** The money ledger is pure `Decimal`: `impact_coeff · participation.powd(impact_exponent)`, no f64 in the value chain. `rust_decimal-1.42.1` `powd→checked_powd→exp(exp·ln)` is a pure-Decimal Taylor series with zero f64/f32/libm → byte-identical arm64↔x86_64. Confirmed empirically by the golden reproducing over irrational `participation^0.5` costs. Capacity's `f64::powf(1/β)` feeds only 12dp-`hash_stable`-rounded sealed weights, never the hashed ledger.
